Website Critiques

Questions:

  1. What was your first impression of this site?
  2. How does this site organize the flow of information? (Do they have links on the left and text on the right? Some other scheme? No scheme?)
  3. Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?
  4. Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?
  5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?
  6. Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?
  7. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the
  8. navigation well done?
  9. Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?
  10. How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?
  11. What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?

Website 1 Critique - Jami Lin

  1. My first impression of this website is that there it has a lot of links around the page.
  2. There are links all over the page, seems to be no flow of information. I do not see a scheme
  3. The site structure confuses me. All of the pages have too much information, I do not know where to start.
  4. The site graphics detract from it. It makes me feel lost because there is so many things on the page already.
  5. The site loaded quickly for me, it was reasonable
  6. Most of the text was readable, but there was some text that were behind buttons. Also the amount of text on each page did not make it easier to read.
  7. It did have a navigation tool, but I do not think it was well done.
  8. It did have a rainbow color scheme on the main page, but not for the others.
  9. It did not have a responsive design.
  10. The site had a busy site design, which is unusual.

  3. The site structure makes sense to me. It is simple and defined throughout it.
  4. The site graphics enhances from it. It is revelant and does not make it too busy.
  5. The text is a good size and easy to read.
  6. The site has a navigation bar that showed up on every page. I think it was well done.
  7. The home page loaded quickly, it was reasonable.
  8. The site did have a color scheme that stayed consistent throught.
  9. This site had a responsive design.
  10. The site had a simple easy to follow design very well done.
